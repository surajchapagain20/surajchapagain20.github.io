About

It’s now almost a decade that photography has been a way of seeing the world for me. As an experimental photographer I am just trying to click the shutter to delve into my surroundings regardless of paths and styles of photography. I’ve always been fascinated by ‘Natural Scene’ and what it contains including Hills, Landscape, Rivers, bridges, Monuments, and most importantly,the cause of its existence: Natural Beauty.
